  • 2 min read
  • 0 Comments
  • 0

Applications are invited for the post of Software Engineer at Nokia Bengaluru. Apply by April 14!

About Nokia

Nokia is committed to innovation and technology leadership across mobile, fixed and cloud networks. Your career here will have a positive impact on peopleâ€™s lives and will help us build the capabilities needed for a more productive, sustainable, and inclusive world.

We challenge ourselves to create an inclusive way of working where we are open to new ideas, empowered to take risks and fearless to bring our authentic selves to work

Job Description

Highly motivated DevOps engineer with 4+ years of experience in R&D. Expertise in implementing and maintaining automated build and deployment pipelines, optimizing build times and resource usage. Strong understanding of CI/CD methodologies and Git branching strategies. Seeking to contribute to a team focused on innovative solutions and leveraging AI/ML for improved efficiency.

Responsibilities

  • Contribute expertise: Leverage my experience in Yocto, Jenkins, Gerrit, and other build tools to streamline and optimize the build process.
  • Improve efficiency: Implement caching strategies, parallelization, and configuration tuning to reduce build times and resource usage.
  • Enhance reliability: Proactively monitor build pipelines, investigate failures, and implement solutions to improve reliability and efficiency.
  • Automate data pipelines: Utilize AI/ML algorithms to automate and optimize data-driven pipelines, improving data processing and analysis.
  • Collaborate effectively: Work closely with the team to understand their needs and contribute to a collaborative and efficient work environment.
  • Embrace new technologies: Stay up-to-date with the latest advancements in DevOps, CI/CD, and AI/ML, actively seeking opportunities to learn and implement new technologies.
  • Share knowledge: Actively participate in knowledge sharing sessions and contribute to the team’s overall understanding of best practices and innovative solutions.
  • Continuous improvement: Embrace a culture of continuous improvement, constantly seeking ways to optimize processes and enhance the overall effectiveness of the team.

Requirements

  • Build Automation: Yocto, Jenkins, Gerrit, distCC, ZUUL
  • Scripting and Automation: Python, Groovy, Shell
  • CI/CD: Proven experience in designing and implementing CI/CD pipelines
  • Git: Experience working with Git technology and understanding of Git branching strategies

Desirable

  • Containerization: Docker, Kubernetes (EKS), OpenShift
  • AI/ML: Exposure to AI/ML algorithms
